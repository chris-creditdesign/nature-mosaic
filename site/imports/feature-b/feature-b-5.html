Tyrannosauroid Tree

The tyrannosauroid superfamily includes Cretaceous tyrannosaurids, such as T. rex, and more distant relatives that first emerged in the Jurassic period. Researchers are trying to trace how tyrannosauroids evolved from small early species to the giants of the Cretaceous.
